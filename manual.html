▲ Management System Every barrier must be governed by one of the four elements below
In bow tie risk assessment, each barrier links to a management system element that governs its operation, maintenance and performance standard. The coloured top stripe on each barrier pill in the diagram shows this link. Degradation Controls are governed by the same element as their parent barrier.
📋
Policy
High-level commitment and intent. E.g. HSE Policy, Process Safety Policy, Asset Integrity Policy.
