⚠ Safety Critical Elements & Tasks Auto-flagged via human-involvement risk matrix
Safety Critical Elements (SCEs) are barriers whose failure could lead to a MAH — shown by red corner badges (H/M) on barrier pills. Safety Critical Tasks (SCTs) are human tasks for operating, maintaining, testing or recovering those SCEs — listed below. Click any SCT for a full HSE proforma SCTA via Claude.
Build your bow tie above, then SCEs and SCTs will be auto-identified.
▲ Management System Every barrier must be governed by one of the four elements below
In bow tie risk assessment, each barrier links to a management system element that governs its operation, maintenance and performance standard. The coloured top stripe on each barrier pill in the diagram shows this link. Degradation Controls are governed by the same element as their parent barrier.
📋
Policy
High-level commitment and intent. E.g. HSE Policy, Process Safety Policy, Asset Integrity Policy.
0 barriers
🔧
Activity
Specific tasks maintaining barrier integrity. E.g. Inspection rounds, maintenance tasks, operator checks.

Procedure
Step-by-step instructions governing barrier operation. E.g. Permit to Work, ESD Procedure, MOC.

Standard
Technical / engineering standards. E.g. API 510, NFPA 13, ISO 45001, company engineering standards.
